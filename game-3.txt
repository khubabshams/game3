Game 3 – Promotional Event DetectionObjective: 
1. Analyze the correlation between variables
2. Detect the promotional weeks for each combination of product and client
Available data:
• Historical series of sales units (volume and value) for each product and client
• Prices (for each Standard Unit or sales unit)
• Distribution of sales in each client’s store (weighted distribution)

For a product, weighted distribution compares the sales value of pharmacies selling to the sales value of the pharmacy total
